General Info for Buisness Analyst

Description:  understands the business requirements for this project to be successful,
sustainable, scalable. Manages that revenue aspects are taken into account and
implemented. This means: how do you make money with this product, how do you measure
success and establish a representation in code for such metrics. Contributes to the
customer development the product manager needs to perform (interviews and alike) to
recognize/identify business opportunities. Works with the CEO to secure funding with an
SBIR proposal
